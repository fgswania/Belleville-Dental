Belleville Dental Clinic offers a wide range of comprehensive dental services, including preventive care, general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, restorative dentistry, orthodontics, pediatric dentistry, and oral surgery. Our dedicated team is equipped to handle all aspects of your dental needs under one roof.

It is generally recommended to visit the dentist for regular check-ups and cleanings every six months. However, the frequency may vary depending on your oral health needs. Our experienced dentists will assess your individual situation and recommend a suitable schedule for your routine dental visits.

At Patel Dental Clinic, we accept various payment options to make dental care accessible for our patients. We also accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards for payment.

We offer a range of cosmetic dentistry services to help you achieve the smile of your dreams. Our treatments include teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, dental bonding, and orthodontic options such as braces and clear aligners. Schedule a consultation with our cosmetic dentists to discuss your goals and explore the best options for enhancing your smile.
