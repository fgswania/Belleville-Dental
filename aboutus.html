Know More About Dental Services


Dental Care

Dental care is medical care and hygiene relating to your teeth. This coverage will help pay for dental treatment and allow you to make decisions about your dental care based on need rather than cost.

Oral Surgery

Oral surgery refers to any surgical procedure performed on your teeth, gums, jaws or other oral structures.Oral surgery is usually performed by an oral and maxillofacial surgeon or a periodontist.

Root Canal

A root canal is performed when the endodontist removes the infected pulp and nerve in the root of the tooth, cleans and shapes the inside of the root canal, then fills and seals the space.


Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is ideal for people who have healthy, unrestored teeth (no fillings) and gums.

Orthodontics

Orthodontic treatments, such as braces or aligners, correct misaligned teeth and bites.

Extractions

A tooth extraction may be necessary for many reasons, including severe damage or decay.
